Investor


Investors can invest low amounts from COP $100 to COP $400,000 or even more, this is what will support the loans that will be used and paid by the rappitenderos, our investors will obtain a percentage of the payments made by the rappitenderos

Rappitendero

The rappitendero can request a loan to buy a motorcycle, this loan can be paid by making installments and at the time of requesting it, it will be studied to verify the feasibility of lending him money

Administrator

He is the one who can offer a proposal to our rappitendero or reject the request, he will be responsible for sending you the amount of loan installments you are requesting and the interest rate you must pay.

About our project


This project started as an idea to expand the area where the rappitenderos sent their orders, later we saw a background and consulting with several people we noticed that we offered a better job opportunity for our rappitenderos, as such we offered much more security and wanted to offer not just a business idea, but an opportunity to get people ahead and have the purchasing power to buy not only a work implement, but a means of transportation that in our opinion is much cheaper than public transportation.

Regarding the idea of ​​the investor, we wanted to generate the same trust in him, we see this person as that person who would support us and that we would reward for having placed their trust in us, and together with them we could improve the quality of life of many people, we know the subject of investments and we know that not all of us have exorbitant amounts of money to invest, so these people could generate extra income no matter how small they might be and thus together help each other as a family, it should be This app is to improve people's lives, rather than being a portfolio project.
